Frequently Asked Questions

How Do I Earn Points?

Points are given based on a few criterias:
1. Number of successful participations.
2. Winning competitions being hosted on COMP2GO.
3. Hosting well-attended competitions by others.
4. Best improvement in terms of skill-level


Cancellation Policy

We understand that sometimes unforseen circumstances can occur which would lead to an absense for an upcoming event. Don't worry, we got your back. Simply cancel the event at least 3 days before to ensure that your account won't be penalized. After 3 account strikes, we will conduct an internal check and take appropriate actions where necessary.
